About Tinuola Media Group

Tinuola Media Group is a dedicated platform focused on providing up-to-date news and information on the activities and accomplishments of Honorable Abdulazeez Yakubu Aremu (Tinuola), the Chairmanship Candidate for Irepodun Local Government Area under the All Progressives Congress (APC).

About Hon. Abdulazeez Yakubu Aremu (Tinuola)

Born on July 6, 1976, Honorable Abdulazeez Yakubu Aremu, popularly known as Tinuola, is a committed public servant with a passion for fostering positive change within his community. A native of Irepodun Local Government Area in Kwara State, Tinuola has a rich background in education and public administration, with over a decade of experience serving the public.

Vision and Core Values

Hon. Abdulazeez is driven by a vision to build a more inclusive and prosperous Irepodun Local Government, where every individual has the opportunity to thrive. His core values include:

  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and strong moral principles in all actions.
  • Transparency: Ensuring openness in decision-making processes.
  • Equity: Promoting fairness and justice for all.
  • Community: Building strong, supportive, and engaged communities.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and new ideas to solve local challenges.

Education and Career

Hon. Abdulazeez holds a National Certificate in Education from Kwara State College of Education, Oro. His dedication to public service began in 2007 when he was elected as a Councillor representing Omu-Aran Ward II at the Irepodun Local Government Council. Since then, he has held various leadership roles, including Chief Whip of the House, Executive Vice Chairman of Irepodun Local Government Area, and most recently, Party Chairman of the APC in Irepodun LGA.

Key Achievements

Throughout his career, Hon. Abdulazeez has been committed to improving the lives of the people in his community. Some of his notable achievements include:

  • Empowering local artisans through donations of tools and equipment.
  • Supporting education by providing bursaries to students and donating educational materials.
  • Promoting local infrastructure development by constructing roads, culverts, and community facilities.
  • Facilitating community empowerment programs for youth and women.

Policy Focus

As he campaigns for the Chairmanship position, Hon. Abdulazeez remains committed to addressing key issues such as:

  • Education: Ensuring every child has access to quality education.
  • Healthcare: Expanding healthcare services and making them affordable for all.
  • Economic Development: Creating jobs and supporting small businesses.
  • Environmental Protection: Implementing sustainable practices to preserve our environment.

Community Engagement
