Personal Profile of Hon. Abdulazeez Yakubu Aremu (Tinuola)

Chairmanship Candidate, All Progressives Congress (APC), Irepodun Local Government Area

Personal Information

  • Date of Birth: 6th July, 1976
  • Marital Status: Married
  • Nationality: Nigerian
  • State of Origin: Kwara
  • Local Government Area: Irepodun
  • Hobbies: Travelling, Politics, Leadership, Learning, and Researching
  • Languages: Yoruba, English

About Me

I am Honorable Abdulazeez Yakubu Aremu (Tinuola), a dedicated public servant with a passion for creating positive change in our community. With a background in education and over a decade of experience in public administration, I have devoted my career to advocating for the rights and well-being of our citizens.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Transparency
  • Equity
  • Community
  • Innovation

Education

  • National Certificate in Education: Kwara State College of Education, Oro, Kwara State, Nigeria
  • Senior Secondary School Certificate: West African Examination Council, Nigeria
  • First School Leaving Certificate: Muslim Community L.G.E.A. School, Omu-Aran, Kwara State, Nigeria

Political Career

  • 2007-2010: Elected Councillor to represent Omu-Aran Ward II at the Irepodun Local Government Council
  • 2007-2010: Elected Chief Whip of the House in Irepodun Local Government Council
  • 2010-2013: Elected Executive Vice Chairman of Irepodun Local Government Area, Kwara State
  • 2019: Appointed as a Member of the Kwara State Handing over and Transition Committee
  • 2021-2024: Elected as the Party Chairman of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Irepodun Local Government Area, Kwara State; resigned in 2024 to contest at the APC local government elections

Other Career Information

  • CEO of AREMAZIZ Nigeria Limited
  • 2023: Elected State Chairman of Motorcycle Operators Union of Nigeria (M.O.U.N), Kwara State Chapter
  • 2023: Elected National Financial Secretary of Motorcycle Operators Union of Nigeria (M.O.U.N)

Key Achievements

As Irepodun Local Government Vice Chairman:

  • Donated motorcycles to the commercial motorcycle riders' association in Irepodun
  • Donated sewing machines to the Fashion Designers' Association in Irepodun
  • Donated clippers and other equipment to the Barbers' Association in Irepodun
  • Made personal donations to ward residents including fans, generators, pressing irons, and DVD players in Omu-Aran
  • Constructed an Archway Gate as an entrance to the Palace of HRM, The Olomu of Omu-Aran
  • Constructed a culvert on the Omu-Aran Township road
  • Renovated T-junction road direction signs at various junctions in Omu-Aran
  • Awarded bursaries to students from selected secondary schools in Omu-Aran
  • Made a cash donation to a church for land purchase
  • Donated an auto fault detector to mechanics in Irepodun
  • Empowered several youths in Irepodun

As Councillor Representing Omu-Aran Ward II at the Irepodun Local Government Council:

  • Renovated community market stalls in Omu-Aran
  • Empowered widows in Omu-Aran Ward II
  • Made personal donations of tables, chairs, and benches to market women in Omu-Aran II
  • Donated a delivery bed to Ihaye Community Health Center
  • Donated overhead tanks to Rabba and Ilaro Community Mosque and Afin
  • Distributed clothing materials to residents of Omu-Aran Ward II during various festive periods
  • Provided roofing materials to churches in Omu-Aran Ward II

Vision and Goals

My vision is to build a more inclusive and prosperous community where every individual has the opportunity to thrive. I aim to:

  • Enhance public safety through community policing initiatives
  • Promote economic growth by supporting local businesses
  • Improve access to quality healthcare and social services
  • Improve food production by supporting and empowering farmers
  • Reduce the number of out-of-school children and improve the quality of education

Policy Focus

  • Education: Ensuring every child has access to quality education
  • Healthcare: Expanding healthcare services and making them affordable for all
  • Economic Development: Creating jobs and supporting small businesses
  • Environmental Protection: Implementing sustainable practices to preserve our environment

Community Engagement

I believe in the power of community and have worked closely with various organizations to address local issues. My efforts include organizing town hall meetings, participating in community clean-up events, and partnering with non-profits to provide resources to those in need.
